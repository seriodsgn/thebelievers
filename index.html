Group members

People returning home go through a wide range of thinking and emotional issues. Our programs are designed by those who have faced the same issues and have solutions that work.

How it works

You can choose the program that you want to attend. You create your program from our list of services.  You will be assigned a person who can coach you through the process.

Program duration

The length of each program varies depending on the topic. Also, you have the option to repeat a session or request special help. 

First results

The first results of each program is stability and direction to a solution. We work with to determine the best course of action for you.

Our mission is to
help restore you to your life!

MKE Blocks' primary mission is to educate, facilitate, collaborate, and cooperate for the betterment of all lives within the community. We educate our clients using programs that are designed exclusively to support their mental, physical, and spiritual well-being.

Our programs and services are located in the same building to facilitate an easier process for our clients. We collaborate with our clients to develop a range of responses.to issues identified through the assessment process.
