About Us

The Youth Cultivation Initiative is a program
designed exclusively to support the interest,
health and safety of our youth. Our program
aim is to nurture creativity by providing
opportunities through Expressive Arts for all
young people aged 12 â€“ 25.

We are committed to:

"Building A Vision Our Youth
Can Internalize and Believe In"

The Youth Cultivation Initiative is located in a
great place for learning and to develop new
skills. Youth have a chance to get involved in
an art, music or recreational activity, be part of a workshop, or use the facilities to conduct an exhibition, and so much more

The Youth Cultivation Programs consist of a host of therapeutic right brain activities essential to healthy development and a balanced life. These activities have been taken out of our youth schools
because of budget cuts. Knowing the significance of these activities, Art professionals in an effort to
cultivate the youth in our community are offering the following Expressive Arts Programs.
